Reproducible Steps

Follow the steps below without refreshing your browser window.

1. Open browser console. 2. Tap on 'Wistia Video' tab at the bottom. 3. Notice console.log message 'I got a handle to the video!'. 4. Tap the 'Home' tab. 5. Tap the 'Wistia Video' tab again. 6. Notice the 'I got a handle to the video!' onReady event fires twice. 7. Then, each additional time you go back and forth again (without refreshing the browser), another onReady event is fired each time.
